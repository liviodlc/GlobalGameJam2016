How to Launch the Game:

1.	Install the .apk on two Android devices

2.	On a computer, go to http://ggj2016game.cloudapp.net/
	On the bottom left corner of the page, click End Game.
	
3.	Launch the app on both android devices.

4.	Fight! First one to complete all steps wins!

How to Play Again:

1. Shutdown the app on both Android devices.

2. On your computer, click End Game.
